About MK electricals and Control Limited

Founded in 2005, MK electricals and Control Limited has grown from a small local electrical contractor to a respected provider of comprehensive electrical services across the region. Our journey has been marked by a steadfast commitment to excellence, continuous learning, and adapting to the evolving needs of our clients and the industry.

What sets us apart is our team of highly skilled and certified electricians who bring both technical expertise and a customer-focused approach to every project. We invest heavily in ongoing training and the latest technology to ensure we can deliver the most advanced and efficient solutions to our clients.

Safety is at the core of everything we do. We maintain rigorous safety standards and hold all necessary certifications and insurance to protect both our team and our clients. Our quality assurance processes ensure that every job, no matter how small, meets our high standards before we consider it complete.
